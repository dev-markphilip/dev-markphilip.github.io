EduCredPH

Dec 2024 - Current

System Architect & Full-Stack Developer

Designed, developed, and tested a proof-of-concept academic credentialing platform as part of my dissertation.

  • Architected and developed EduCredPH, a blockchain-based system for issuing, verifying, and managing academic credentials securely and transparently.
  • Utilized Hyperledger Fabric, Node.js, and RESTful APIs to implement secure, permissioned access to student records.
  • Designed a proof of authority (PoA) model allowing designated institutions to issue credentials, while users verify them without third-party intermediaries.
  • Demonstrated how blockchain can prevent tampering and enhance auditability in academic records — solving problems like credential fraud and inefficient verification processes.
  • Successfully tested endorsement policies and transaction flows in a multi-org setup using Docker and Fabric CLI tools.

Online Student Admission System

Dec 2019 - Oct 2024

Software Engineer (All Phases)

A custom web application designed to digitize and streamline the student application and enrollment process for an educational institution. I independently handled the full software development lifecycle — from client interviews and system design to development, deployment, and ongoing support.

  • Developed a full-stack web application using PHP and MySQL, enabling students to submit applications, and track their admission status online.
  • Built a dynamic admin panel for admissions officers to review applications, update statuses, and manage applicant data in real-time.
  • Integrated email notifications to automatically inform applicants about updates, reducing manual communication by over 80%
  • Improved the admissions processing time by up to 70% compared to manual paper-based workflows.
  • Implemented role-based access control to ensure secure and appropriate access levels for students, admins, and staff.

HRIS - Attendance Management System

Sept 2018 - Feb 2020

Software Engineer (HRIS Solution)

A PHP-powered HR tool integrated with biometric attendance systems, designed to simplify HR workflows, automate DTRs, and support payroll with accurate employee work summaries.

  • Designed and developed a full-featured Attendance Management System integrated with a fingerprint-based biometric scanner to automate employee time logging.
  • Implemented DTR (Daily Time Record) generation with automatic computation of tardiness, undertime, and overtime based on configured business rules.
  • Developed summary reports of hours rendered by employees, exported and submitted directly to the accounting department for payroll processing.
  • Helped reduce manual logging errors and improved payroll accuracy until its discontinuation in 2020 due to COVID-related changes in work setup.
  • Enabled role-based access control, allowing HR, admin, and department heads to access appropriate modules securely.
