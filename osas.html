Online Student Admission System

A full-stack, end-to-end admissions management system deployed across 10 university campuses. It unified workflows from application to enrollment, integrating the Admission Office, University Infirmary, and Registrar's Office into a continuous digital process.

Project Description

Developed a centralized, paperless student admission system deployed across 10 campuses, serving over 100,000 applicants. The platform transformed traditional paper-based processes into a streamlined digital workflow, significantly improving application tracking and scheduling efficiency.

The system achieved an 80% reduction in CHED billing preparation time by automating reporting workflows and implementing intelligent appointment scheduling features, while maintaining rigorous data security standards.

Key Features & Responsibilities

  • Multi-Campus Deployment: Successfully implemented system across 10 campuses with centralized management
  • Process Automation: Automated reporting workflows and CHED billing preparation
  • User-Centric Design: Developed applicant dashboards with real-time status updates
  • Administrative Tools: Built comprehensive admin review modules for application processing
  • Security Implementation: Ensured secure data entry and protection of sensitive applicant information

Project information

  • Project Duration 2019-2024
  • Impact: 100,000+ applicants served, 80% faster billing preparation
